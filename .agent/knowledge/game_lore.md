# 🍒 GAME LORE & AESTHETICS (Project Atmosphere)

This document defines the "soul" of Mark Vinicius Cherry Tycoon. Agents MUST refer to this to avoid hallucinating the game's tone, narrative, or aesthetic direction.

## 🎭 The "Feel" & Tone
- **Tone**: A "Vibe-First" world. It should feel alive, breathing, and highly kinetic. Not just a flat dashboard, but a tactile diorama.
- **Player Motivation**: Building a mechanical/organic hybrid empire where agricultural rhythm meets brass-punk industry.

## 🌍 The Lore & Setting
- **Setting**: A "Neo-Steampunk" reimagining of rural Poland (Opole/Namysłów/Głubczyce). Brass tractors, steam-powered cherry shakers, and glowing botanical laboratories.
- **Narrative Hooks**: The tension between natural organic growth (the cherries) and industrial scaling (the gears and steam).

## 🎨 Visual & Audio Aesthetics
- **UI Palette**: "Neo-Steampunk Cherry" — Brass, tarnished copper, glowing ruby reds, and deep atmospheric shadows.
- **Animations (Juice)**: Hyper-tactile. Screen shakes, petal bursts, whip pans, and cinematic fades. Interactive elements must bounce and react ("Haptic Visuals").
- **Lighting**: Dynamic Atmospheric. "God Rays" piercing through leaves, and a persistent "Golden Hour" warmth over the farm.
- **Depth**: High-parallax scrolling and glassmorphic panels to create 3D illusion.

## 🚫 Anti-Patterns (What NOT to do)
- 🚫 DO NOT use flat, unshaded colors or generic flat-design dashboards.
- 🚫 DO NOT use instant cut transitions between screens (always fade or pan).
- 🚫 DO NOT implement buttons without reactive micro-animations.
